Jupiter

Overview

Jupiter is the 5th planet from the Sun and the largest in our Solar System. It is a gas giant known for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm system that has lasted for centuries.

Key Facts

  • Distance from Sun: 778 million km
  • Orbital Period: 11.86 years
  • Rotation Period: 10 hours
  • Diameter: 139,820 km
  • Moons: 95+ (Largest: Ganymede)

Fun Facts

  • Jupiter is so massive that it influences the orbits of other planets.
  • Its Great Red Spot is a storm larger than Earth.
  • Jupiter has a faint ring system, discovered by Voyager 1 in 1979.
  • It has the strongest magnetic field of any planet in the Solar System.

Jupiter's Great Red Spot

Jupiter’s Great Red Spot is a giant storm that has been observed for over 300 years.
